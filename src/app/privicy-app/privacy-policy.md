## 1. Information Collection & Use

### a. Personal Data
While using our Service, we may ask you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information that can be used to contact or identify you ("Personal Data"). This includes:
*   Email address
*   First name and last name

### b. Usage & Log Data
We may also collect information that your mobile device sends whenever you visit our Service. This may include your IP address, device type, unique device identifiers, and other diagnostic data to ensure app security and core functionality.

## 2. Permissions & Audio Data
To provide the core language coaching features, we request **Microphone (RECORD_AUDIO)** permission.
*   Audio is processed using **Google ML Kit** for real-time pronunciation feedback.
*   We do not store audio recordings permanently on our servers unless specifically required for a requested support case.

## 3. Retention of Data
We will retain your Personal Data only for as long as is necessary for the purposes set out in this Privacy Policy. *   **Google Play Services:** App distribution and core Android APIs.

## 5. Children's Privacy
